Notre Histoire

Our Story

Tout a commencé dans une cuisine familiale.

It all began in a family kitchen.

Long before Mala became a brand, it was simply the blend prepared for family meals, celebrations and gatherings.

Roasted and ground by hand, each batch followed the same recipe carefully refined over decades.

What began in one Mauritian kitchen became a tradition shared across generations. Today, that tradition continues exactly as it always has: patiently, carefully and without compromise.

La Collection

The Collection

Trois mélanges. Une tradition.

Three blends. One tradition.

Each Mala blend begins with the same philosophy: carefully selected spices, roasted with patience and blended with purpose.

Whether bold, gentle or celebratory, every blend carries the character of a recipe refined over decades in Mauritius.

  • Masala Grillé

    Roasted Spice Blend

    The original Mala blend. Rich, aromatic and deeply versatile, it is crafted to complement everything from everyday family meals to special occasions.

  • Masala Sans Piment

    Roasted Blend Without Chilli

    All the warmth and fragrance of our signature blend, without the heat. Balanced, approachable and full of character.

  • Épices pour Briani Mauricien

    Mauritian Style Briani Blend

    Created for one of Mauritius' most celebrated dishes. A fragrant blend designed to bring depth, richness and celebration to the table.
